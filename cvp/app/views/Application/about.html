About Us

Ozonetel pioneers in providing hardware and software platform that delivers complex Business Telephony Solutions in easy way. "KooKoo" Ozonetel's PSTN cloud platform empowers Enterprises to quickly deploy cost-efficient and feature rich Telephony Solution for their business. With KooKoo enterprises can enable multi-location/multilingual IVR, Contact Centre, PBX and other telephony services with localised call treatment, with zero hardware installation and managing all using online admin portal. KooKoo enhances customer experience and reduces operation cost.
